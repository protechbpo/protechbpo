Refund Policy

At Link Next, we strive to ensure the satisfaction of our customers. As an authorized dealer and intermediary for third-party service providers, our refund policies are aligned with those of the providers we represent. Refunds may be issued under specific conditions as outlined below:

Eligibility for Refunds

  1. Cancellation Before Installation
    If you cancel your service request before installation or activation, you may be eligible for a full refund of any upfront payments made to Link Next, provided the service has not yet been executed.
  2. Service-Related Issues
    In the event of a verifiable issue with service activation or installation that is directly attributable to Link Next, we will make reasonable efforts to resolve the issue. In rare cases, and at our sole discretion, a partial or full refund may be processed.
  3. Non-Refundable Charges
    Certain fees are non-refundable, including but not limited to:
    • Installation charges
    • Activation fees
    • Administrative and execution costs
    These are service-related operational expenses and are not eligible for refunds.

  1. Submit a Request: Contact our customer service team within 14 days of the issue or cancellation date.

Additional Notes
